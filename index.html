Articles

Cybersecurity

Protect your devices

Cybersecurity means protecting computers, networks, and personal data from online threats like hacking, malware, and phishing.

  • Use strong passwords and unique accounts.
  • Keep software updated and patched.
  • Be cautious with email links and downloads.

Recommended tools: Password Generator Tool and Virus Scanner.

Shortcuts

Work faster with shortcuts

Keyboard shortcuts help you complete tasks quickly without moving your hands from the keyboard. They improve speed and efficiency for everyday activities.

  • Ctrl + C copies selected text.
  • Ctrl + V pastes copied content.
  • Ctrl + Alt + S or Ctrl + Alt + R can launch your shutdown and restart shortcuts.
Malware

Understand malware risks

Malware is software designed to damage systems, steal data, or gain unauthorized access. It can slow down your device, cause failures, or expose private information.

  • Run antivirus software to stay protected.
  • Avoid unknown downloads and suspicious attachments.
  • Update your system regularly for better security.

Some malware is meant as a harmless prank, which is why we have a dedicated Prank Malware category.
